# Overview

**SSH Sentinel** is a telemetry-driven honeypot that captures and analyzes SSH attack activity. Deployed on Azure to study real-world intrusion behavior.

---

## Results

- Collected **200K+ SSH attack logs** over several weeks
- Parsed and normalized into structured telemetry
- Visualized attack sources with Power BI
- Extracted geo-location and frequency distributions
